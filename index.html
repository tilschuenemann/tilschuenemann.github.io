Bring Back Spotify Album Codes!

Spotify launched their codes in ~2019 and now there are many services to leverage this feature, enabling you to get a sticker of your favorite songs code among other products like key fobs, mugs, shirts. The code can be scanned by the Spotify App and will play the scanned song / album / artist. I’ve seen posts on social media where people would print an albums cover with the corresponding code and hang it onto their walls....
